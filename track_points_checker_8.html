REVUP — spacePts checker & track builder

Check an existing board’s space centres, or build a new circuit from a template and export it straight into index_20.html. Corner lines are drawn before their at index — crossing into that space triggers the check.

Change Spaces any time — the points are re-spread evenly along the shape you’ve dragged, and corners keep their position round the lap.

Tents

CornerxyRot
One marquee per corner — it holds that corner's Road Condition token. Drag the tent to move it, drag its ▲ knob to spin it. Corner is the space its line sits before.

Gravel & Tunnels

Pick a brush, then tap or sweep spaces on the board to paint them. Tap a painted space again to undo it. In = the Spot nearest the Race Line, Out = the outside Spot — gravel can sit on either or both. Tunnels cover whole Spaces and are stored as runs, so ranges wrap past the start line (70-4 is fine). Typing in the boxes works too: 12-15, 22.

Move & export

Tick Move whole track above, then drag anywhere on the board to slide the whole lap around.
